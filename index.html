Three Little Pigs

Chapter 1
Once upon a time there were three little pigs who decided to build their own houses to protect themselves from the big bad wolf.
Chapter 2
The three little pigs proudly admired their houses. They build a straw house, wooden house and the third with his brick house. in a bush, the wolf watched them silently.
Chapter 3
The big bad wolf came and saw the straw house. He blew and blew, and the straw house flew into the air. The first little pig ran to his wooden brother's house.
Chapter 4
The wolf went towards the wooden house and huffed and puffed, and the wooden house also collapsed.
Chapter 5
After their houses were destroyed, both little pig brothers decided to go to their third brother's brick house.
Chapter 6
The wolf came to the brick house, he blew with all his might, but the house was too strong and he couldn't knock it down.
Chapter 7
The wolf climbed the house and jumped through the chimney, the little pigs had prepared a trap, the wolf fell into a pot of boiling water and later shot out of the house.
Chapter 8
The little pigs celebrated for having been able to free themselves from the danger of the wolf
